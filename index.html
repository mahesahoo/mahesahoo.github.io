About

Bridging Disciplines, Building Systems

I'm pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science at ITER, SOA alongside a BS in Data Science & Applications from IIT Madras — a dual-track approach that reflects how I think: never choosing between depth and breadth when you can engineer both.

My work sits at the convergence of three domains: AI Engineering (building agentic systems that reason and act autonomously), Neuroscience & Psychology (understanding the biological substrate of intelligence), and System Design (architecting frameworks for deliberate human optimization).

I believe constraints are design problems — not permission to settle. Every system I build and every idea I pursue is held to that standard. Think from first principles, then build deliberately.
